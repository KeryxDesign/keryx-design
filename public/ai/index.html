Direct Response Design Methodology

The DRD approach integrates:

  1. Psychology: Understanding buyer behavior and decision-making
  2. Copywriting: Persuasive text that drives action
  3. Visual Hierarchy: Guiding the eye to key messages and CTAs
  4. Testing: Data-driven optimization for better results

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Direct Response Design?

Direct Response Design (DRD) combines visual design with direct response copywriting principles. Unlike traditional design focused on aesthetics, DRD prioritizes measurable results - conversions, leads, and sales.

How is Keryx different from traditional design agencies?

Keryx focuses on results, not just aesthetics. Every design decision is made to maximize the marketing effectiveness of the material, integrating copywriting strategy with visual design.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes. Services are available in Italian and English. The studio is based in Rome, Italy but works remotely with clients worldwide.

What is a Magalog?

A magalog is a hybrid between a magazine and a catalog - an editorial-style publication that sells products or services through content marketing rather than traditional advertising.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary by project complexity. Landing pages typically take 2-3 weeks, print materials 1-2 weeks, and book covers 1-2 weeks. Larger projects are quoted individually.
